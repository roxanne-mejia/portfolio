ROXANNE MEJIA

ARTIST, GRAPHIC DESIGNER, WEB DEVELOPER

I am a self taught full stack web developer. I gained my core knowledge of web developement from freecodecamp.org. Thanks to freecodecamp.org's great training and certification courses I was able to learn both front end design and back end programming and have received four certificates so far (Responsive Web Design, JavaScript Algorithms and Data Structures, Front End Development Libraries, Data Visualization).
My experience as an artist and poet has better developed my inquisitive nature and understanding of recursion. In my work as a whole, I make an extra effort to understand how concepts work underneath the hood in order to create a beautiful and engaging user experience.
My experience in the resturant industry has made me more resilient and able to work well under pressure and has taught me organization and timing skills but most importantly customer service skills and the importance of teamwork.
